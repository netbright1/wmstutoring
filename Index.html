About WMS Tutoring

Based in Sheffield, WMS Tutoring provides specialized education for logistics professionals and individuals looking to upskill in the warehouse management sector.


We move beyond generic training. Our sessions are tailored to your specific needs, focusing on the practical application of two of the world's leading WMS platforms.

  • Manhattan Associates: Deep dive into configuration, waving, and inventory flows.
  • SSI Schaefer: Understanding automation integration and WAMAS workflows.
  • Practical Focus: Real-world scenarios, troubleshooting, and system optimization.
